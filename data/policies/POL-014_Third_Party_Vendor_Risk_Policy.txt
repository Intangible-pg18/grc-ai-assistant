POLICY ID: POL-014
TITLE: Third-Party Vendor Risk Management Policy
VERSION: 5.2
LAST UPDATED: 2025-01-10
OWNER: Chief Risk Officer
REGULATORY MAPPING: SOC 2 CC9.2, ISO 27001 A.5.19, GDPR Art. 28, CCPA, NYDFS 500.11

PURPOSE:
This policy establishes due diligence, risk assessment, contracting, and ongoing monitoring requirements for third-party vendors to ensure they maintain appropriate security, privacy, and operational controls protecting Diligent and customer data while meeting service level commitments.

SCOPE:
Applies to all third-party relationships including SaaS providers, cloud infrastructure, professional services, contractors, consultants, business process outsourcers, and any vendor accessing Diligent systems, data, or facilities. Excludes immaterial vendors (<$25K annual spend with no data access).

VENDOR RISK CLASSIFICATION:

Tier 1 - Critical: Vendors processing customer data, providing critical services (cloud infrastructure, authentication), or with production access. Extensive due diligence and continuous monitoring required.

Tier 2 - High: Vendors with access to confidential data or important business functions. Standard due diligence and annual reviews.

Tier 3 - Medium: Vendors with limited data access or standard business services. Streamlined assessment and biennial reviews.

Tier 4 - Low: Minimal risk vendors with no data access. Basic assessment at onboarding only.

VENDOR LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT:

Pre-Engagement Assessment:
- Business case and vendor selection criteria documented with competitive evaluation
- Security questionnaire (Tier 1: SIG Core, Tier 2: Custom 100-question, Tier 3: 25-question)
- Privacy assessment including data processing activities, subprocessors, and cross-border transfers
- Financial stability review examining financial statements and credit reports for Tier 1/2 vendors
- References checked with minimum 2 customer references for Tier 1 vendors
- Onsite visits conducted for critical vendors processing customer data

Due Diligence Requirements by Tier:
- Tier 1: SOC 2 Type II report, ISO 27001 certificate, penetration test results, BCP test results, insurance certificates ($5M cyber liability minimum)
- Tier 2: SOC 2 or ISO 27001, security questionnaire, insurance certificates
- Tier 3: Security questionnaire, basic attestations
- Tier 4: Vendor attestation of security practices

DETAILED CONTRACTUAL REQUIREMENTS:

- Data Processing Addendum (DPA) with Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data transfers
- Security and privacy obligations aligned with Diligent's policies and regulatory requirements
- Right to audit vendor controls annually with 30-day notice
- Incident notification within 24 hours of discovery with root cause analysis within 72 hours
- Subprocessor approval process requiring prior written consent
- Service Level Agreements (SLAs): 99.9% uptime for critical services with financial penalties
- Data return and destruction procedures upon contract termination within 30 days
- Liability caps, indemnification, and insurance requirements ($2M general liability, $5M cyber)
- Business continuity and disaster recovery capabilities with RTO/RPO commitments

ONGOING MONITORING:

- Annual vendor risk reassessment for Tier 1/2 vendors with updated security documentation
- Continuous monitoring of vendor security posture using SecurityScorecard/BitSight ratings
- Quarterly business reviews with Tier 1 vendors covering performance, security, and roadmap
- Vendor incident tracking with impact assessment and corrective action verification
- Contract renewal process includes risk reassessment and business value evaluation
- Vendor termination procedures with data retrieval, access revocation, and knowledge transfer

VENDOR INVENTORY:
Centralized vendor register maintained in GRC platform with 200+ vendors, tier classifications, contract details, risk scores, and review dates.
